About Me

Welcome to my portfolio, which serves as both a mirror and a map: reflecting who I am and charting where I aim to go. At its core, my journey weaves through the realms of Electrical and Computer Engineering and Exercise Science, reflecting a unique confluence of technology and health. This portfolio is not just a showcase of my skills but a testament to my passion for utilizing technology to improve physical well-being and prevent sports injuries.

My academic pursuits—majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering with a minor in Exercise Science—lay the groundwork for my overarching goal: to meld the precision of engineering with the dynamism of physical health. This amalgamation drives my interest in exploring how innovative solutions can safeguard athletes, enhance performance, and revolutionize the approach to sports injury prevention.

Through this portfolio, I invite you to journey with me through the exploration of possibilities that lie at the nexus of engineering and exercise science. It's a portrayal of my academic interests, my professional ambitions, and, fundamentally, who I am: an individual passionate about leveraging technology to foster healthier, more resilient athletes.
