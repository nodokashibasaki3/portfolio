Schwarze Welt: Chopin Inspired Waltz

This project presents a new waltz inspired by the romantic style of Frédéric Chopin, focusing on the innovative use of nonharmonic tones to enhance melody and harmony, similar to techniques used by Chopin. Alongside the composition, the project includes an explanation on how nonharmonic tones—such as passing tones, suspensions, and appoggiaturas—are utilized to create tension and emotional depth within the piece. This approach combines a practical demonstration of music composition with theoretical insights, aiming to both celebrate Chopin's influence and explore advanced musical concepts in a straightforward manner.
